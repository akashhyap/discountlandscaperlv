Frequently Asked Questions

How much does landscaping typically cost in Las Vegas?

Landscaping costs vary widely depending on the scope of your project, materials chosen, and the size of your property. Basic landscape renovations may start around $5,000, while comprehensive projects with premium materials can range from $15,000 to $50,000+. We provide detailed, itemized quotes so you understand exactly what you're paying for.

How long does a typical landscaping project take to complete?

Project timelines depend on the complexity and scope of work. Small projects like artificial turf installation might take 2-3 days, while complete yard renovations could take 2-3 weeks. During your consultation, we'll provide a realistic timeline for your specific project.

Do you offer financing options?

Are you licensed and insured?

What areas do you serve?
