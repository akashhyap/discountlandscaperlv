What You Need to Know ABOUT PAVER INSTALLATION

Pavers are versatile, durable hardscape elements that can transform your outdoor living spaces while adding significant value to your property. At Discount Landscaper LV, we specialize in custom paver installation for patios, walkways, driveways, and pool decks throughout the Las Vegas Valley. Our skilled craftsmen combine quality materials with expert installation techniques to create beautiful, long-lasting hardscapes that enhance both the functionality and aesthetics of your outdoor areas.

Why Choose Pavers?

Pavers offer numerous advantages over poured concrete or asphalt surfaces, making them an excellent investment for your home or business property. Their modular nature allows for creative design possibilities while providing practical benefits that other hardscape materials simply can't match.

Benefits of Paver Installation:

  • Durability: Properly installed pavers can last 25-50 years or more, outperforming concrete and asphalt in the harsh Las Vegas climate.
  • Low Maintenance: Pavers require minimal upkeep and can be individually replaced if damaged, unlike concrete which often requires complete section replacement.
  • Design Flexibility: Available in countless shapes, sizes, colors, and textures, pavers allow for unlimited design possibilities and custom patterns.
  • Safety: Paver surfaces provide better traction when wet compared to poured concrete, making them safer around pools and in areas with frequent foot traffic.
  • Weather Resistance: Pavers handle the extreme temperature fluctuations of Las Vegas better than concrete, with significantly less cracking and heaving.

Our Paver Installation Services

At Discount Landscaper LV, we offer comprehensive paver installation services tailored to your specific needs and property characteristics.

Custom Patterns

We work with you to design custom paver patterns that complement your home's architecture and landscape design. From traditional herringbone and basketweave to modern linear arrangements and custom inlays, we can create a unique look that reflects your personal style.

Quality Materials

We use only premium paver products from trusted manufacturers, offering a wide selection of materials including concrete pavers, natural stone, porcelain, and clay brick. Our experts will help you select the right material for your specific application, considering factors like traffic, exposure, and aesthetic preferences.

Proper Drainage

Proper water management is critical in hardscape installation, especially in Las Vegas where occasional heavy rains can cause flooding. Our installation process includes careful grading and drainage planning to ensure water flows away from your home's foundation and prevents pooling on your paved surfaces.

Professional Installation

Our installation process follows industry best practices to ensure a beautiful, long-lasting result:

  • Thorough site preparation and excavation
  • Installation of proper base materials and compaction
  • Precise paver cutting and placement
  • Edge restraints to prevent shifting
  • Joint sand application and compaction
  • Optional sealing for enhanced protection and color enhancement
